Loyola Team Meeting - Demo Questions (Kwara TPR 2020-2025)
============================================================

DEMO FLOW: Upload data, then ask these in order.


PART 1: SET THE SCENE (you ask these)
--------------------------------------
1. What are the TPR trends from 2020 to 2025?
2. Is malaria positivity increasing or decreasing across Kwara State?
3. Which LGAs have gotten worse over time?


PART 2: DIG DEEPER (you ask these)
------------------------------------
4. Which LGAs improved the most and which improved the least?
5. How do facility-level trends compare across LGAs?
6. What is the year-over-year change in TPR for each LGA?


PART 3: OPEN FLOOR (invite Bashir and team)
---------------------------------------------
Let them ask their own questions. If something fails,
rephrase and try again.

Suggested prompts to encourage participation:
- "Bashir, is there a specific LGA you want to look at?"
- "Are there any wards you've been concerned about?"
- "Any questions about a particular year or time period?"


QUESTIONS TO AVOID (known issues)
----------------------------------
- "How has malaria testing changed over time across LGAs?" (plotting error)
- "Break down the TPR trends by age group" (insufficient data points)
